When only one of overflow-x or overflow-y is set and the other is static then the other computes to auto and browsers decide what to do (most allow scrolling)

Here the container has set width and height, plus overflow-x: hidden. therefore the overflow-y gets computed to auto and the overflowing content gets hidden and is reachable scrolling
